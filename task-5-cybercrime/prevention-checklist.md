**Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)**

* Always verify the sender before clicking on links in emails or messages.
* Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone.
* Check website URLs carefully (look for “https://” and avoid suspicious links).
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.
* Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
* **UPI Safety:** Never approve unknown payment requests or “collect requests.”
* **UPI Safety:** Double-check the recipient’s UPI ID before sending money.
* Do not download apps or files from untrusted sources.
* Log out from shared or public devices after use.

**Report Cybercrime:**

* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline Number: **1930**
