# Social Media Checklist for College Students

## Do’s:

* Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts.
* Think before posting; ensure your content is appropriate.
* Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings.
* Share accurate and verified information only.
* Use social media to build your personal and professional network.

## Don’ts:

* Do not share offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
* Avoid posting confidential or sensitive information.
* Do not engage in online arguments or trolling.
* Avoid oversharing personal details publicly.
* Do not spread misinformation or rumors.
